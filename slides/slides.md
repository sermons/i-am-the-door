## The Door (Jn10)
1. False Shepherds (v1-4)
1. True Blessings (v4-10)

---
**Truly**, truly, I say to you, he who <br/>
does not enter the sheepfold by the **door** <br/>
but **climbs** in by another way, <br/>
that man is a **thief** and a robber. 

But he who enters by the door <br/>
is the **shepherd** of the sheep. <br/>
To him the **gatekeeper** opens. 

[John 10:1-2 (ESV)](# "ref")

---
The sheep hear his **voice**, and <br/>
he calls his own sheep by **name** <br/>
and **leads** them out. 

When he has brought out all his own, <br/>
he goes **before** them, 

[John 10:3-4 (ESV)](# "ref")

---
and the sheep **follow** him, <br/>
for they **know** his voice. 

A **stranger** they will not follow, <br/>
but they will **flee** from him, for <br/>
they do not know the **voice** of strangers.

[John 10:4-5 (ESV)](# "ref")
